App Settings

Notifications:

  • Turn push notifications on or off as well as sound and vibration.
  • Set visual alert when new content becomes available.
  • Choose a quiet time when notifications will be silenced.

My Profile: Enter your name, email address and phone number. This information only prepopulates forms on the app. It is not used in any other way to identify you. Contact forms may be sent anonymously at your discretion.

Share: Share this app with others to download.

Data Sync: Displays latest date and time the app updated information. You may sync immediately for the latest information. The app automatically syncs each time it is opened.

Test: Use test to verify that your device is receiving audible push notifications.

Messaging Opt-In/Opt-Out: Enter your name, phone number and check the apps to receive text messages when sent by them. You will be sent a "One Time Passcode" (OTP) to verify your approval. You may opt-out at any time by unchecking the box.

Content Full Screen: The app's content displays full screen by default. However, if you prefer to have the header and app buttons display above the content, uncheck the box.

Event: By checking the box you change the calendar default setting to view as a list of events. Left unchecked the calendar view will display.

Passcode: Available only when a Private Call/Msg. Module Tab is present on the app. The passcode can be used to prevent unauthorized use of the Private Call/Msg. Tab.
